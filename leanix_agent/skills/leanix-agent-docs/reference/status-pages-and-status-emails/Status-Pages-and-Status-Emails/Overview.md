##  Overview
There are several options to check the availability of SAP LeanIX services and learn about planned maintenance and incidents.
Resource | Usage Notes
---|---
[Public Status Page ![Information published on SAP site](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/themes/sap-light/img/sap_link.png)](https://help.sap.com/docs/link-disclaimer?site=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sap.com%2Fabout%2Ftrust-center%2Fcloud-service-status.html%3FoverlayDataCenterId%3DXMS%26overlaySolutionId%3DNZA753%26productName%3DSAP%2520LeanIX "https://www.sap.com/about/trust-center/cloud-service-status.html?overlayDataCenterId=XMS&overlaySolutionId=NZA753&productName=SAP%20LeanIX") at SAP Cloud Services Trust Center ![Calendar view showing service history and a service degradation on one day](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/loioe12efdecadc542f3851d4a347f9bb53b_HiRes.png) |  Public page.  There is no configuration possible.
[Availability Page![Information published on SAP site](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/themes/sap-light/img/sap_link.png)](https://help.sap.com/docs/link-disclaimer?site=https%3A%2F%2Fme.sap.com%2Fsystemsprovisioning%2Favailabhility "https://me.sap.com/systemsprovisioning/availabhility") in SAP for Me ![View of SAP for Me showing the Availability page and an opened screen showing details of a specific status metric](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/loio4e7377c0975442f79237974b278744b7_HiRes.png) |  Available to users with an SAP for Me account who have one of the following permissions:

  * Super administrator

[Status Emails![Information published on SAP site](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/themes/sap-light/img/sap_link.png)](https://help.sap.com/docs/link-disclaimer?site=https%3A%2F%2Fme.sap.com%2Fsystemsprovisioning%2FgetNotified "https://me.sap.com/systemsprovisioning/getNotified") ![Email with a status notification from SAP for Me indicating that a planned maintenance has been completed](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/loiod337ce3bd11f40dc82430f6cfdb9d74b_HiRes.png) |  Available for users with and without an SAP for Me account.

##  Overview
Key performance indicators (KPIs) are metrics calculated using your fact sheet data. They help you measure and evaluate performance across various aspects of your enterprise architecture landscape. Use KPIs to gain actionable insights into your organization's strategic goals and operational efficiency.
KPIs appear on dashboards, giving you visibility into key metrics and trends over time. You can use KPIs to:
  * Monitor the health of your application portfolio
  * Track progress toward strategic objectives
  * Identify trends and patterns in your enterprise architecture landscape
  * Make data-driven decisions about technology investments
  * Demonstrate value to stakeholders

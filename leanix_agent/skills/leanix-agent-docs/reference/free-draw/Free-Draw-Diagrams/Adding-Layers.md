##  Adding Layers
Adding layers enables you to visualize different levels of abstraction within your architecture diagrams. You can use layers to represent various levels of your architecture to make complex diagrams easier to understand and navigate. Reducing visual clutter helps you focus on specific aspects of your architecture without being overwhelmed by details.
To add layers, select Layer from the menu at the top and add the layer by clicking the '+' icon on the modal. You can toggle the layers to view or hide by selecting the eye icon.

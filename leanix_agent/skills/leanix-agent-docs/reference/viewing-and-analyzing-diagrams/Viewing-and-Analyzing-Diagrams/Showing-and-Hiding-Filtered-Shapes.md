##  Showing and Hiding Filtered Shapes
When you apply filters to a diagram, you can control whether filtered‑out shapes remain visible or are hidden from view.
To adjust filtered shape visibility:
  1. Apply a filter to your diagram.
  2. In the toolbar, choose the show/hide filtered out shapes toggle.
  3. Select how filtered items appear.
     * Show filtered out shapes (default): Non-matching fact sheets and relations remain visible at 50% opacity, preserving context and relations.
     * Hide filtered out shapes: Only matching fact sheets and relations appear, reducing visual clutter.
When filtered items are hidden, a banner shows the number of hidden shapes and provides an option to temporarily reveal them.

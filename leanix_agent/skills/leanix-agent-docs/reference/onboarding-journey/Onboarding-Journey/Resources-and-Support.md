##  Resources and Support
Use the following resources and support channels to get additional information about SAP LeanIX:
  * Q&A sessions: Join our onboarding Q&A sessions with customer success managers to address specific questions and learn from others' inquiries. These sessions help you gain foundational knowledge about SAP LeanIX.
  * [Onboarding resource center![Information published on SAP site](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/themes/sap-light/img/sap_link.png)](https://help.sap.com/docs/link-disclaimer?site=https%3A%2F%2Fsupport.sap.com%2Fen%2Fproduct%2Fonboarding-resource-center%2Fleanix.html?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "https://support.sap.com/en/product/onboarding-resource-center/leanix.html"): Access essential resources to prepare for, start, and succeed in your onboarding journey.
  * [Documentation](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix "https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix"): Visit our documentation to explore use cases, tutorials, and detailed feature guidance. Documentation is crucial during onboarding, as it provides links to detailed topics and step-by-step instructions.
  * [Community![Information published on non-SAP site](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/themes/sap-light/img/3rd_link.png)](https://help.sap.com/docs/link-disclaimer?site=https%3A%2F%2Fcommunity.leanix.net "https://community.leanix.net"): Engage with the SAP LeanIX community to share best practices, connect with peers, and interact with our team. The community serves as a central knowledge platform, offering access to resources and documentation articles.
  * [SAP Learning![Information published on SAP site](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/themes/sap-light/img/sap_link.png)](https://help.sap.com/docs/link-disclaimer?site=https%3A%2F%2Flearning.sap.com%2Fproducts%2Fbusiness-transformation-management%2Fleanix "https://learning.sap.com/products/business-transformation-management/leanix"): After onboarding, explore SAP Learning for advanced training and certification programs. This will help you enhance your expertise and discover more complex topics and features.
  * [Support](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/contact-support?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "If you encounter issues, have questions, or need assistance with SAP LeanIX, our support team is here to help. You can contact support directly within SAP LeanIX or submit a request in the SAP for Me portal."): If you encounter issues, have questions, or need assistance with SAP LeanIX, our support team is here to help. You can contact support directly within SAP LeanIX or submit a request in the SAP for Me portal.

##  Who to Involve
Involve relevant teams in the onboarding process to ensure efficient collaboration:
  * Core team: Engage your core team early and bring in additional stakeholders as you progress. Include colleagues who primarily work with SAP LeanIX from the start.
  * Senior sponsor (management/C-level): Secure support from a senior sponsor, such as a VP or C-level executive, with the authority to drive adoption. Their responsibilities include:
    * Sponsoring the rollout of SAP LeanIX in the organization, including calls to action and reminders for data input.
    * Helping identify and convince relevant users and groups to participate.
    * Defining clear objectives and business purposes for SAP LeanIX.
  * Fact sheet owners (contributors to data): To build a complete view of your enterprise landscape, you’ll need to collect information from experts, such as application or business owners, who update and maintain fact sheet data.


Align with your senior sponsor in a meeting or kick-off to plan the long-term vision for SAP LeanIX. Focus on the following:
  * Secure their support across the organization, including active engagement of users in collecting and maintaining data.
  * Get an overview of current priorities and potential needs from the enterprise architecture team.

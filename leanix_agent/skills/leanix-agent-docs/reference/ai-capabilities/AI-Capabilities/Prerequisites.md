##  Prerequisites
Agreeing to SAP AI terms for LeanIX is a prerequisite for activating any of the LeanIX AI capabilities. The SAP AI terms acknowledge that SAP services may use AI functionality to deliver certain features and are part of SAP's standard commercial terms
  * SAP AI terms are included for new customers.
  * If you haven’t signed your contract and want to activate AI capabilities, contact your SAP Account Executive to sign the SAP AI terms and start the activation process.

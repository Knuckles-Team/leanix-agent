##  Why Is AI Governance Relevant?
As AI technologies become increasingly integrated into various aspects of business operations, the need for structured AI governance frameworks becomes essential to ensure ethical use, regulatory compliance, and alignment with strategic objectives. Clear AI governance practices help organizations align their AI initiatives with business goals, manage risks, and comply with evolving regulations.
AI governance enables organizations to:
  * Align AI initiatives with business strategy to support informed decision-making and strategic planning.
  * Ensure ethical practices and regulatory compliance to mitigate legal, reputational, and operational risks.
  * Establish standards for responsible and effective AI adoption to maximize the value of their AI investments.
  * Reduce shadow AI and vendor sprawl by standardizing the usage of AI technologies.
  * Gain a competitive advantage by enabling safe, scalable experimentation and deployment.
  * Facilitate cross-functional collaboration among stakeholders, such as business, IT, and legal, to ensure AI initiatives deliver maximum business value.

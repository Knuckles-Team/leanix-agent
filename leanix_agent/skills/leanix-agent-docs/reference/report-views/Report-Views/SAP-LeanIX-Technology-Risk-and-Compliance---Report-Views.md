##  SAP LeanIX Technology Risk and Compliance - Report Views
In the SAP LeanIX Technology Risk and Compliance, three additional views are available for landscape, matrix, roadmap, and radar report. It includes:
  * Obsolescence: Mitigated Risk Percentage
  * Obsolescence: Missing Data Percentage
  * Obsolescence: Unaddressed Risk Percentage


To learn more about these views, see [Technology Obsolescence Risk Views in Reports](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/technology-obsolescence-risk-statuses-and-views-in-reports?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "Learn how risk statuses are determined for IT components and applications and understand reporting views, such as Missing Data Percentage, Mitigated Risk Percentage, and Unaddressed Risk Percentage.").

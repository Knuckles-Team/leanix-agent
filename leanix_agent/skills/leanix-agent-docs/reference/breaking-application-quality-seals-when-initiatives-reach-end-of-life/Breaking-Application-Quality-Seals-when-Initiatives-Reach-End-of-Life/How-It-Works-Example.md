##  How It Works: Example
Scenario 1: Initiative Reaches End of Life
The initiative "App modernization" reaches End of Life on January 15. A nightly check on January 16 triggers automation 1. All applications linked to "App modernization" have their quality seals broken.
Scenario 2: Application Linked to an End of Life Initiative
The application "CRM system" is linked to the initiative "Legacy migration," which is already at End of Life. Automation 2 triggers immediately and breaks the quality seal on "CRM system."
Scenario 3: Multiple Initiatives (Any Logic)
The application "Finance dashboard" is linked to three initiatives: one at End of Life and two active. The quality seal is broken because one initiative is at End of Life.

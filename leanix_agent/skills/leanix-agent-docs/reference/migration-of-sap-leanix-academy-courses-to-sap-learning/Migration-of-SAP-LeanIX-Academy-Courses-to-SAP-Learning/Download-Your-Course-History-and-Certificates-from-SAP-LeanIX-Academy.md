##  Download Your Course History and Certificates from SAP LeanIX Academy
  1. Log in to your account on SAP LeanIX Academy.
  2. On the user profile menu, choose My Profile.
![User profile menu expanded on SAP LeanIX Academy homepage, showing "My Profile" and "Sign Out" options.](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/loio6c99eee48de748cb85ddfa8408034fc6_LowRes.png)
Logging in to SAP LeanIX Academy
  3. Your profile shows your learning history. Take a screenshot or download a copy of this page to export your course history.
![User profile page in SAP LeanIX Academy showing learning paths and course registrations.](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/loio2f248fde9bba4661b47f7dba0819d49e_LowRes.png)
Course History in SAP LeanIX Academy
You can download certificates for the “Practitioner Level 1” and “Practitioner Level 2” courses directly from this page.
![Certificates section with “View certificate” and “Add to profile” buttons for two courses.](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/loio19b714ac01774b3d9f815c61bd34425f_LowRes.png)

##  AI Agent Governance Reportţéł
If you use the [AI Agent Hub](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/ai-agent-hub?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "Discover and manage AI agents across the organization."), you can track the approval status of AI agents segmented by application type with the predefined AI Agent Governance report. You can find this report in the AI Agents collection on the reports page.
Use radar rings to visualize the Approval State, and adjust the Ring Category to explore different dimensions of the data. The report indicates which AI agents are in Draft, Preview, Accepted, or Rejected status.
When AI agents are in the Draft state, collect key attributes for further assessment: usage, AI risk, business value, and the potential of related business capabilities. Once you've assessed these key attributes, move the status to In Preview, then accept or reject AI agents based on your defined criteria. For example, accept AI agents with low risk, high usage, and high business value.

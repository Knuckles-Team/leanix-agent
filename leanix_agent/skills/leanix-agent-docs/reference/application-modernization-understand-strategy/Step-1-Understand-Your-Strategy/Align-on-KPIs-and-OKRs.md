##  Align on KPIs and OKRs
Once stakeholders are identified and involved, collaborate with them to define how you want to measure success. Establish clear, business-driven KPIs and OKRs that reflect both technical progress and business impact. Examples include:
  * Percentage of applications migrated to cloud platforms
  * Reduction in legacy system usage
  * Time-to-market improvements
  * Cost savings or efficiency gains


Capture these in SAP LeanIX using the [Objective Fact Sheet](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/objective-modeling-guidelines?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "Learn how to model objectives. This includes best practices and applicable use cases, and common antipatterns."), and link them to relevant initiatives and business capabilities.

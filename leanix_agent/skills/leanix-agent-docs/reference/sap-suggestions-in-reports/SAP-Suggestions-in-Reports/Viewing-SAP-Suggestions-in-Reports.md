##  Viewing SAP Suggestions in Reports
SAP suggestions are displayed on the fact sheet tile and in the drill-down in reports. Icons indicate the suggestion status:
  * ![](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/loio8794a2fcf1cf487aadb0e1ee8da90b05_LowRes.png) Suggestion not in use: No fact sheet has been created from this suggestion.
  * ![](https://help.sap.com/doc/72d375467c1e4dcb872dfa2998b6328d/CLOUD/en-US/loio51ebb6834acc42dea955033f46974085_LowRes.png) Suggestion in use: A fact sheet was created from a suggestion at least once in the workspace. In business capabilities where suggestions haven’t yet been used, suggestions show additional optimization opportunities.

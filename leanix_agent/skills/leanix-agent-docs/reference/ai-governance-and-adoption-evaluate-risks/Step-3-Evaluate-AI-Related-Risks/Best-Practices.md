##  Best Practices
  * Prioritize applications and AI agents with high risk, high usage, and high potential: These should be your top priority because they have the potential to deliver significant value but also pose risks. They require robust governance policies and risk mitigation strategies to ensure they are managed effectively and safely.
  * Address applications and AI agents with high risk and unknown or low usage: These represent a significant risk. They could be shadow technologies that need to be brought under governance. Investigate these promptly to understand their impact and bring them under control to prevent any potential issues.
  * Review applications and AI agents with minimal risk, high potential, and active usage: These are your champions because they're delivering high value with minimal risk. They should be used as models for adoption in other parts of your organization, demonstrating how to effectively use AI technologies while maintaining safety and compliance.

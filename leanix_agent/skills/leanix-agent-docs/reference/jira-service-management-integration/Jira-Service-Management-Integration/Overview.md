##  Overview
The integration between SAP LeanIX and Jira Service Management enables bidirectional synchronization of IT asset and business application data. Jira Service Management is Atlassian's IT service management platform that IT and support teams use for tracking and resolving issues with IT assets. The platform maintains a CMDB containing IT asset information essential for enterprise architecture and obsolescence risk management.
The integration synchronizes CMDB data between the systems, providing enterprise architects with visibility into the IT landscape and application dependencies. IT assets from Jira Service Management appear as IT component fact sheets in SAP LeanIX, including all relevant relations to other components such as applications and hardware. Dependencies identified in Jira Service Management are automatically documented in SAP LeanIX.

##  Benefits
  * Visualize and communicate architecture: Create as-is and target architecture diagrams to illustrate data flow, dependencies, and relations. Embed diagrams in presentations to engage stakeholders and support informed decisions.
  * Explore and customize: Drill down into details, explore dependencies, and customize diagrams by adding shapes or images, even for elements not represented in the inventory.
  * Ensure consistent, up‑to‑date architecture views: Access, create, and link fact sheets directly within diagrams to keep architecture and inventory data in one place. Receive notifications about inventory changes and choose when to refresh your diagram.
  * Define reusable rules for data visualization: Use diagram templates to create standardized diagrams that reflect the current state of your inventory. Create fixed, editable diagrams when you need point‑in‑time documentation, sharing, or audits.
  * Track and share progress: Save different versions of diagrams to capture architecture over time. Include them in dashboards, fact sheets, and presentations to share insights and monitor changes.

##  Managing Fact Sheet Mappingsî‚ˆ
You can manage fact sheet mappings on the integration configuration page.
  * Edit a mapping: Hover over a mapping, then adjust the parameters you need.
  * Activate or deactivate a mapping: Use the toggle on the left to activate or deactivate a mapping.
  * Delete a mapping: Hover over a mapping, then choose Delete Mapping on the right. Deleting a mapping does not delete synchronized data. Existing fact sheets remain in both systems.

##  Onboard Fact Sheet Owners
Prepare relevant communications for fact sheet owners and inform them about their core tasks in SAP LeanIX. Ensure they understand their key responsibilities and know how to use the tool.
### Onboarding Resources
To onboard fact sheet owners, you can share the following resources with them.
Getting started:
  * [Getting Started](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/getting-started?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "Introductory guide to begin your journey with SAP LeanIX."): An overview of SAP LeanIX and its key features.
  * [SAP LeanIX for Application and Business Owners](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/for-application-and-business-owners?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "First steps in using SAP LeanIX for application and business owners."): A getting started guide for fact sheet owners describing the key operations they perform.
  * [Navigating SAP LeanIX](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/navigation?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "Overview of the SAP LeanIX user interface."): An overview of the SAP LeanIX user interface.
  * [SAP LeanIX Resources](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/resources?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "In addition to product documentation, there are several other resources to help you to get the most out of SAP LeanIX."): A list of resources with additional information.


Data collection and maintenance:
  * [Fact Sheet Subscription](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/fact-sheet-subscription?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "Fact sheet subscription assigns responsibility and accountability to users for maintaining data. Learn about fact sheet subscriptions, including types, roles, and how to assign and subscribe to fact sheets to promote stakeholder involvement and ensure data accuracy and completeness."): A subscription is a mechanism for assigning responsibility and accountability to users for maintaining fact sheet data. Share information about the configured subscription roles and associated responsibilities.
  * [Quality Seal](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/quality-seal?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "Quality seal ensures data integrity by assigning approval responsibility to accountable and responsible subscribers of fact sheets. When broken, it prompts verification and approval of fact sheet information."): A quality seal ensures the overall integrity and quality of data on fact sheets. When the quality seal is broken (based on the renewal interval or due to data changes), fact sheet owners get notified to review and update fact sheet data.
  * [Adding and Editing Data in Fact Sheets](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/adding-and-editing-data-in-fact-sheets?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "Users with the appropriate permissions can add and edit data in fact sheets, while administrators can customize and manage these permissions. By switching to table view in the inventory, you can perform inline editing across multiple fact sheets, and using the import option, you can update multiple fact sheets in bulk."): Fact sheet owners review, update, and verify information on fact sheets.
  * [Responding to Surveys](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/responding-to-surveys?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "Responding to surveys is crucial for providing feedback on enterprise architecture. Access surveys via the collaboration tab, fact sheets, or dashboard. Learn how to fill them and provide your insights."): Surveys are used for collecting data from multiple users. Fact sheet owners receive notifications prompting them to respond to surveys. Once they submit a response, the corresponding fact sheet fields are updated.
  * [To-Dos](https://help.sap.com/docs/leanix/ea/to-dos?locale=en-US&state=PRODUCTION&version=CLOUD "To-dos facilitate task delegation, collaboration, and governance and can be used to ensure data integrity and quality through transparent task management."): To-dos are used for assigning tasks and responsibilities to fact sheet owners, such as updating fact sheet data or approving the quality seal.
